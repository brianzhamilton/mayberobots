RPG Mayhem (Coming Soon!)

What's more fun the simulating a fantastical reality with your friends? Watching others do it!

In RPG Mayhem, we three goof around wandering the world of our fantasy RPG exploring the depths and art of human storytelling.

Book of the Week (Coming Soon!)

In this tamer podcast series, our hosts read one book every week and give you the low down on what's great and not-so-great about said book. Genres can vary from fiction to nonfiction, from sci-fi/fantasy to horror to psychological thriller.

If you have a great book in mind that you'd like to hear us talk about, head to our contact page and give us your suggestions!
